Software Development Cell(SDC)

Tech Club

Software Development Cell, USICT is responsible for the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University's constant growth towards the aim of becoming a digitally dependent organisation. Students of the university have been selected to develop different software for helping out the faculty as well as students in day to day college activities. Some of the ongoing projects of SDC are Student Feedback System, a feedback management system used by 5000+ students and 150+ teachers of the university. Also, the University Website has also been revamped by SDC recently and is a work in progress. There are many more such projects in line for SDC to solve the real life problems faced by administration, faculty and students.
